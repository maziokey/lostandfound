About L + F

Lost and Found is a haven for lost objects. Users can post objects which they found and the owners can contact the users to pick up the lost object. All you need is a picture of the lost object and a short description of the object, where you found it and where you want the owner to come and pick it up. Users have to find a way to verify the the lost object belongs to the rightful owner if contacted. So what are you waiting for? sign up and join the L + F family.
